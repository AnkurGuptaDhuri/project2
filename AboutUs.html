About Us

Join to produce Fresh and Healthy Air

Grow Plants, Grow Oxygen

Vision: To produce our own oxygen before inhaling it. we must grow plants in our houses so that we can produce clean and fresh air for ourself. Every Person should balance the consumption and production of oxygen. How to balance.

Need of this platform: It's moral duty of every person that he/she produce the oxygen before he/she inhales that oxygen. To grow Oxygen, we should grow Plants and Trees . This Platform will share the insight and best methods to grow popular plants/trees etc in India. This will also help us to know about the plant lovers near us. Plants lovers can share the plants, seeds, cuttings, bulbs etc in FREE of cost with each other. It will also overcome the problem of buying expensive plants. All plants multipli/propagate themself with seeds, cuttings, bulbs etc. We just need to share and provide little space for them to grow. Register
We can also buy plants from near by registered nurseries or gardeners from your area.

Benefits of Plants:

  • Purifies the air inside the house in Free of cost - Indoor plants
  • Provides fresh oxygen to our body.
  • Provide fresh aroma and positive Energy
  • Keep global warming in control.
  • Saves from soil erosion. Due to warm temperature in India, Soil creates air pollution.
  • Provides wood, food, flowers, medicines etc
